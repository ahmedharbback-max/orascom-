About The Project

A Masterpiece
on the Red Sea

Makadi Heights is a one-of-a-kind integrated resort in Makadi Bay, Hurghada. Built by real estate giant Orascom Development, this mega-project is an ultimate multicultural, lavish, and perfectly equipped destination — elevated 78 meters above sea level for breathtaking 180° panoramic sea views.

Designed in partnership with world-renowned EDSA, with only 12% of the land built up and 88% dedicated to lush landscaping, pools, and water features, Makadi Heights redefines coastal living.
